About Aryan

Aryan Institute of Engineering and Technology (AIET), the top engineering college in Odisha and one of the best Btech college in Bhubaneswar. Established in the year 2009 this top college in Odisha is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to BPUT, RKN, Odisha, AIET has made significant progress in the area of imparting high quality technical education and research. ARYAN ranked 2nd among 'best Btech/engineering colleges in Odisha' and 39th amongst 'top private engineering colleges in India' in a survey conducted in the year 2020. The Alumni of AIET are well placed in various Corporate/Industries both in India and abroad.

Our Focus

Shaping a Future of Success.

Best Engineering College

AIET is one among the best 10 Engineering Colleges of the Odisha State, and is also the most preferred institution for aspiring students and their parents. AIET has earned itself the reputation of being a centre of academic excellence in many ways over the years.

Highly Qualified Faculty

AIET has highly qualified and professionally trained faculty members who impart Interactive Communication skills, Technical and Employability skills to students, so that they compete successfully in the Global job market.

Green Campus

Modern infrastructure, comprising ample built up area, well equipped laboratories, libraries and information centres with digitalization and online transaction facilities are integral to the world class amenities provided on campus.

Placements

AIET has an excellent placement track record of students who have been hand-picked consistently by the world's best MNCs like Infosys, Amazon, IBM, HCL, Google Maps, HSBC Bank, ICICI Bank, etc. Aryan provides students plenty of opportunities to work in global markets.

Life Skills & SDL

At AIET Group of Educational Institutions, we are imparting skills (life & professional skills) to the students through Experiential Learning/ Activity Based Learning methodologies. Life Skills are those skills which enable an individual to be more competent in dealing with the day to day challenges in a positive way.

Entrepreneurship Development Cell

Entrepreneurship Development Cell was established in the college with the vision “Turn around the “Job seekers” into “Job Creators” in the year 2011. Currently the AICTE Sponsored IIPC and ED Cells are working. EDC has 1450 Sft space and its own audio-visual equipment.
